Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Vartalabh. Your privacy matters to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when you use the Vartalabh application, website, or related services.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account on Vartalabh, we may collect basic information necessary to operate the service. This may include your email address, username, profile information, and any data you voluntarily provide inside your profile.

Because Vartalabh is a messaging platform, messages you send or receive through the application are stored temporarily or permanently depending on the feature design. This allows conversations to appear correctly between users.

We may also collect limited technical information such as device type, IP address, application version, and usage logs. This information helps us maintain system security and improve performance.

2. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to provide and maintain the service. Without certain information, basic features such as login, messaging, and profile display would not function.

We may use collected data for the following purposes:

• Creating and managing user accounts • Enabling messaging between users • Improving system performance and reliability • Preventing abuse, spam, or security threats • Developing new features and improvements

3. Data Storage and Security

User information is stored using secure backend infrastructure and database systems. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, it is important to understand that no system connected to the internet can guarantee absolute security. While we work to protect your information, we cannot promise perfect protection against all possible threats.

4. Third-Party Services

Vartalabh may rely on third-party technologies or infrastructure providers to operate the application. These services help store data, manage authentication, and maintain server operations.

Such providers only process the information necessary for the service to function and are expected to follow industry-standard privacy practices.

5. User Control and Data Rights

Users maintain control over their own profile information. Within the application you may update profile details, modify account information, or delete messages where features allow it.

If you wish to request account deletion or removal of personal data, you may contact us through the official Vartalabh website or support channel.

6. Data Retention

We keep user data only for as long as it is necessary to provide the service. If an account is deleted, associated personal information may be removed from our systems within a reasonable timeframe unless required for security or legal reasons.

7. Children's Privacy

Vartalabh is not intended for young children. Users should meet the minimum age requirement defined by local digital regulations. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

Continued use of the service after policy changes means you accept the updated version.

9. Contact Information
